TAKE A NEW
APPROACH

Arms length too often describes the relationship between recruiters and clients and candidates. We take a new approach. Focused on understanding our clients’ business, their talent needs and integrated into the communities where talent resides we grow business by raising the bar on talent.

Call it ‘Recruitment +’ or snout to tail, we call it the way we work.

SERVICE OFFERINGS

Recruitment

Recruitment doesn’t start with posting a job advert. Let us help you ensure that the real needs of the position, of your organization and the markers for success are understood. We’ll find the candidates who best represent your unique needs and just as importantly add to and match your team culture.

Selection

Let us systematically take you through a selection process that will balance your needs, the realities of the market and guide you to selecting a team member that has the attributes for success.

Onboarding

Onboarding is made up of the first day, week and month basics. Let us get your new team member learning and spooling up on their first day. This let’s you focus on more pressing tasks of really getting them inducted into the organization and learning what differentiates your organization and makes it special.

Talent Advisory

The talent you need to hire is unique to your organization. We can work with you to develop your customized talent strategy and help it evolve as your team does.

ABOUT US

Scott

Scott has a keen interest seeing organizations succeed by recruiting, selecting and, ultimately, retaining talented team members. He has had the chance to work with significant software companies and places great value in synchronizing talent management with team operations and broader HR initiatives.

Starting with a recruitment agency, recruiting contract tech employees for Fortune 500 clients Scott has grown into corporate recruitment and HR management. This has brought him closer to the business and given him the opportunity to drive bottom line outcomes.

Scott pairs his experience with an academic background that includes a BA from Queen’s University, an Ontario Post Graduate Certificate in Human Resource Management and a Master Human Resource Management degree from York University. Outside the office he’s active with his local cycling club, racing, coaching and sitting on the board of directors.

Tonci

Tonci has a keen interest in the digital and technical spaces. He thrives on diving into new technologies and prides himself on not being limited to Boolean search strings, but actually understanding the technologies and trends that he recruits for. Tonci has worked with everything from organizations of 10,000+ to start-ups where your lead developer is also responsible for making sure the coffee machine is on.

Regardless of size, Tonci has spent his career learning the ins and outs of the teams he works with and raising the bar on the talent they hire. Initially starting his career with a recruitment agency and then moving to consulting and corporate staff recruiting, Tonci has succeeded by aligning organizational and candidate needs to grow organizations and their competitive advantage. With more than 10 years experience, Tonci is experienced marrying strategy with process and partnering business with talent acquisition and HR.

Tonci has a BA from the University of Toronto and an Ontario Post Graduate Certificate in Human Resource Management. He keeps abreast of industry and technology trends by being active in the local technology community and spends time outside of work travelling and training for high stakes pub quiz nights.
